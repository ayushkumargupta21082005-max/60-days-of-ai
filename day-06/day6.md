# 🚀 Day 6 – Resume Optimization with Claude AI

## 📌 Objective

Today I learned how AI can optimize a resume for both Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) and human recruiters without adding false information. The goal was to improve formatting, readability, keyword optimization, and professional branding while keeping every claim truthful.

---

# 🛠️ Tool Used

- Claude AI

---

# 📝 Task Performed

- Uploaded my existing resume to Claude.
- Used a detailed ATS Resume Optimization prompt.
- Analyzed my resume for ATS compatibility.
- Improved formatting and recruiter readability.
- Optimized keywords for AI Engineer / Software Engineer roles.
- Generated a professional one-page ATS-friendly resume.
- Compared the original resume with the optimized version.

---

# 📋 Prompt Used

## Resume Optimization Prompt

```
You are a Senior ATS Resume Expert, Technical Recruiter, and Professional Resume Writer with experience hiring Software Engineers and AI Engineers at top product-based companies.

Your task is to analyze and optimize my resume for both Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) and human recruiters while keeping every statement 100% truthful.

Never invent projects, achievements, certifications, experience, technologies, metrics, or skills.

Only improve wording, formatting, organization, readability, and ATS compatibility.

Target Role:
AI Engineer / Software Engineer (Internship / Entry-Level)
```

# 💡 Key Learnings

- ATS systems prefer simple and structured resumes.
- A well-organized resume improves recruiter readability.
- Strong action verbs make projects and experience more impactful.
- AI can improve resume quality without adding fake information.
- Professional formatting is as important as technical skills.
- Truthful and concise resumes perform better during the hiring process.
