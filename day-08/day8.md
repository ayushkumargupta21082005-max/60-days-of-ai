# 🌍 Personal Environmental Health Analyzer

A modern, interactive environmental analytics dashboard that helps users understand how **air quality** and **water quality** impact overall health. The application combines environmental data visualization, health risk assessment, and personalized recommendations into a single responsive dashboard.

---

## 🚀 Overview

The **Personal Environmental Health Analyzer** is designed to provide users with actionable insights into their local environment. Using Air Quality Index (AQI), particulate matter (PM2.5 & PM10), and water quality metrics such as TDS, pH, and hardness, the dashboard generates an easy-to-understand Environmental Health Report Card.

The application includes interactive charts, health scores, city comparisons, anomaly detection, and practical recommendations to improve everyday well-being.
