# 🚩 Day 14 — AI Job Red Flag Detector

## 📌 Overview

Today I explored how Claude AI can help job seekers analyze job descriptions before applying.

Instead of only checking whether a role matches my skills, I learned how AI can identify potential warning signs in job postings and company information to support better career decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objective

Create an AI-powered Job Red Flag Detector that analyzes:

- Job Description
- Company Information

and identifies possible hiring risks, workplace red flags, and misleading job postings.

---

# 🧠 Prompt

```text
You are an AI Red Flag Detector for job seekers.

Analyze the Job Description and Company Information.

Identify:

1. Unrealistic Requirements
- Excessive experience for the role
- Too many skills/responsibilities
- Contradictory expectations

2. Toxic Workplace Signals
- Burnout indicators
- "Wear many hats"
- "Fast-paced", "rockstar", "hustle culture"
- Poor work-life balance signals

3. Remote Job Authenticity
- Hidden office requirements
- Relocation expectations
- Misleading remote claims

4. Hiring Risks
- Missing salary information
- Vague responsibilities
- Excessive qualifications
- Suspicious hiring practices

5. Company Risks
- Reputation concerns
- Stability concerns
- Growth or layoff indicators

Output:

Overall Risk Score

Top Red Flags

Positive Signals

Risk Breakdown

Final Verdict

5 Smart Interview Questions
```

---

# 🚀 Key Features

- Detects unrealistic hiring expectations
- Highlights toxic workplace language
- Evaluates remote job authenticity
- Identifies hiring transparency issues
- Generates an overall hiring risk score
- Suggests smart interview questions to validate concerns

---

# 💡 Key Learnings

- AI can assist in evaluating job opportunities before applying.
- Job descriptions often contain subtle warning signs that are easy to overlook.
- Prompt Engineering enables AI to perform structured career analysis.
- AI should support decision-making, not replace personal judgment.

---

# 🛠️ Applications

- Internship Search
- Campus Placements
- Off-Campus Hiring
- Remote Job Evaluation
- Career Planning
