# 🚀 Startup Validation Report Generator

> Transform any startup idea into a professional investor-ready validation report using AI. Analyze market opportunities, competitors, customer personas, risks, and growth strategies—all in one comprehensive report.

---

## 📌 Overview

The **Startup Validation Report Generator** is an AI-powered web application designed to help entrepreneurs, students, founders, and innovators evaluate their startup ideas before investing time and resources.

By answering a few guided questions, users receive a consulting-style report containing market analysis, customer insights, competitor research, business risks, and actionable recommendations. The generated report is structured for direct PDF export and resembles reports prepared by leading consulting firms and venture capital analysts.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Enter your startup details.
2. Describe the problem you're solving.
3. Identify your target audience.
4. Share any existing validation.
5. Select your target market.
6. Generate your startup validation report.
7. Export or save the report as a PDF.
