# 🚀 Day 3 – Role-Based Prompting

## 📌 Objective

Today I learned how assigning different roles to Claude changes the quality, depth, and perspective of AI responses.

---

# 🧠 What is Role-Based Prompting?

Role-Based Prompting means assigning a specific role or persona to the AI before asking your question.

Instead of asking a generic question, you tell Claude who it should act as, such as:

- Software Developer
- Startup Founder
- Product Manager
- HR Manager
- Marketing Expert

This helps Claude answer from that expert's perspective.

---

# 📝 Prompts Used

## Prompt 1 (Without Role)

How can I improve customer retention for my SaaS product?

---

## Prompt 2 (Founder Role)

You are a successful SaaS Founder.

How can I improve customer retention for my SaaS product?

---

## Prompt 3 (Developer Role)

You are a Senior Software Engineer.

How can I improve customer retention for my SaaS product?

---

# 📊 Comparison

## Without Role

- Generic advice
- Broad suggestions
- Limited depth

## Founder Role

- Business strategy
- Growth ideas
- Customer success
- Revenue-focused

## Developer Role

- Technical improvements
- Product optimization
- Performance
- User experience

---

# 📚 Key Learnings

✅ Role defines perspective.

✅ Better prompts produce more useful answers.

✅ Different personas solve problems differently.

✅ Role-Based Prompting improves response quality.
