# 🚀 Customer & MVP Blueprint Generator

> Turn a startup idea into a customer-focused product strategy with AI. Generate a professional Customer & MVP Blueprint including Ideal Customer Profiles, Buyer Personas, Customer Journeys, MVP recommendations, prioritization, pricing hypotheses, and execution plans.

---

# 📌 Overview

The **Customer & MVP Blueprint Generator** is an AI-powered product strategy tool designed for startup founders, entrepreneurs, students, and product managers.

Instead of jumping directly into development, the application helps founders understand **who their customers are, what problems they face, which features matter most, and how to build an MVP efficiently.**

By collecting a few startup details—or using an existing Startup Validation Report—the tool generates a concise, consulting-style blueprint ready for product planning and PDF export.

1. Enter startup information.
2. Provide customer details.

4. Add existing validation (optional).
5. Generate your Customer & MVP Blueprint.
6. Review recommendations.
7. Export as PDF if needed.
